Welcome to Domestic Appliance Centre Ipswich

Serving the local area for over 25 years, customer satisfaction is our number one priority. We are an appliance engineering service, offering repairs and servicing as well as buying and selling appliances at fair prices. We have decades of experience to make sure you receive the best possible service always.

SERVICES PROVIDED

We have a wide range of domestic appliance services we provide, including the following;

  • Domestic appliance servicing and repairs
  • Cookers/Hobs (Gas safe registered so can install for both domestic and commercial)
  • Dishwasher Repair
  • Electric Oven Repair
  • Electric Range Cooker Repair
  • Fridge and Fridge Freezers
  • Installations
  • Refrigeration Repair and Re-gas
  • Tumble Dryer Repair
  • Washing Machine Repair
As well as the above we also buy and sell domestic appliances, so if you are looking to upgrade or replace your appliance, we can help you with that too.
